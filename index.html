What we will expect from you

  • Attendance at nearly all practices and tournaments
    • There will be 1-2 practices per weekend and around 5 tournaments depending on postseason results
    • There is of course wiggle room in some circumstances (weddings, work, etc.), but in general team members should be at every event
    • We have some weekends off to give people time to vacation
  • Fitness, including work outside of practice
  • A willingness to work on weaknesses and consider feedback from others, and also to help teammates improve
  • Respect for your teammates and opponents

What you can expect from the team

  • A fun, high-energy practice atmosphere
  • A comprehensive frisbee education (including handler and cutter defense, cutting technique, lots of footwork, disc skills, and more)
  • A strong drive to compete and win
  • Enough perspective to know that winning shouldn't come at the expense of enjoyment and respect for teammates and opponents
  • A strong social game
